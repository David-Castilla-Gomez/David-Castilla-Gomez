## 👋 About Me

Mechanical engineer turned AI/systems builder. Currently pursuing an **MSc in Industrial Mathematics (M2I)** at UPM, focused on numerical methods, optimization, scientific computing and applied machine learning. I split my time between Model-Based Systems Engineering at **Navantia** (defense industry), open-source AI/systems projects and applied research.

Outside engineering, I ran **ElReyGuiri** (2014–2025), a Formula 1 content brand reaching 525K+ combined followers across YouTube, Twitch, Twitter and Instagram.
